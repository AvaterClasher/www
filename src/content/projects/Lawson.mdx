## Lawson

Lawson is an advanced tool for lawyers that enhances the process of understanding judgments by using machine learning to extract and present critical information. This enables lawyers to quickly grasp the essence of a case and make informed decisions with ease.

[Visit Website](https://lawson.vercel.app)

### Features

- ✅ **Extract Key Information**: Automatically extracts essential details from judgments.
- ✅ **Query Judgments**: Quickly find specific information within judgments.
- ✅ **Faster Understanding**: Gain a clearer understanding of judgments more quickly.
- ✅ **Time Efficiency**: Get to the core of a case without manual reading.
- ✅ **Handle Large Documents**: Summarize or query even 1000-page PDFs in seconds.
- ✅ **Improved Legal Research**: Use machine learning to enhance research efficiency.
- ⏰ **Semantic Search**: Find similar judgments from the Supreme Court dating back to 1985.
- ❌ **No Manual Reading**: Eliminate the need for manual reading to extract key information.
